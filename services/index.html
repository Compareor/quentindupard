FAQ

Common questions about engagements.

What's the typical engagement length?

Anywhere from a single advisory call to a 12-week project. Most GTM and global hiring engagements run 4–8 weeks. Due diligence is typically 2–4 weeks for the intensive window.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — but the deepest value comes when you have early product-market fit and are ready to scale GTM or expand internationally. Pre-PMF positioning work is also possible on an hourly basis.

How quickly can you start?

Discovery calls usually within 48 hours. Most engagements kick off within 1–2 weeks of signed SOW.

Can you work alongside our existing consultants or agencies?

Often yes — particularly for due diligence work where I slot in alongside legal and financial advisors. For GTM and hiring work, I prefer being the lead strategic voice, but happy to coordinate with adjacent partners.

Do you take retainers?

Generally no — I prefer milestone-based engagements with clear deliverables. If a long-term advisory relationship makes sense, we can structure something, but I won't bill for time that doesn't produce value.

What's a discovery call like?

30 minutes, free, no obligation. I'll ask about your situation, what success looks like, and what you've already tried. If I'm the right fit, you get a transparent SOW within a few days. If not, I'll point you in a better direction.
